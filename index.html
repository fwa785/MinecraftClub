What we do...



This Minecraft club is a club for kids who love Minecraft and want to game design/build. We meet weekly and make new minigame ideas and build minigame maps. We also host special events for special occasions and have multiple completed project.

    Our Goals
  • To socialize while playing Minecraft
  • To game design
  • To build maps
  • To host events for special occasions
